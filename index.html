About us


Welcome! Anniella's Leche Flan is a pastry shop located in Espana, Manila. We started the business to introduce and revitalizing 'Original Street Style Donuts'. We started at making Leche Flan and different types of kakanins and now, our shop is known for the trending 'Kayle Donuts' in the country.





So, what are you waiting for? From our Kakanins to Donuts, you can taste and get the affordable, quality, and versatile products that we offer.
